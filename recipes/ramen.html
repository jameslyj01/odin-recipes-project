Easy Beef Ramen Recipe

How to make Ramen at home

Below we will learn how to make ramen at home

Ingredients for Ramen Recipe

  • 1 beef sirloin steak
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon coconut oil
  • 4 Cups of beef broth
  • 2 teaspoons of miso paste
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 packets of ramen noodles
  • Salt and ground pepper

Steps to make the perfect bowl of Ramen

  1. Combine steak and soy sauce and marinate for 2 hours
  2. Remove steak and shake off excess
  3. Heat coconut oil over medium high heat
  4. Cook steak for 3 to 4 minutes per side
  5. Remove steak and allow it to rest for 10 minutes
  6. Combine broth, miso past, garlic and sesame oil in saucepan over medium heat, add ramen noodles and cook until soft
  7. Combine broth, noodles and slice steak to serve
