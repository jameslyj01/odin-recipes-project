Butter Chicken Biryani Recipe

How to make butter chicken biryani at home

Below we will learn how to make the perfect butter chicken

Ingredients to make slow cooked butter chicken

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 large skinless, boneless chicken thigh
  • 1 diced onion
  • 3 minced garlic clove
  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon curry paste
  • 2 teaspoons of tandoori masala
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 15 green cardamom pods
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of low fat plain yoghurt
  • salt to taste

Steps to make slow cooked butter chicken

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Heat butter and vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat
  3. Stir in chicken, onion and garlic for about 10 minutes
  4. Stir in tomato paste, curry paste, curry powder, tandoori masala and garam masala
  5. Pour mixture into a slow cooker; stir in cardamom pods, coconut milk and yoghurt; season with salt
  6. Cook until chicken is tender and sauce, remove cardamom pods before serving
