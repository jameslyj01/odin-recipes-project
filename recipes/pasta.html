EASY HOMEMADE PASTA

How to Make Homemade Pasta

Below you will find out that making delicious pasta from scratch is very simple

Ingredients to make Pasta

  • Flour: 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • Egg: Eggs adds fat and moisture to the dough
  • Oil: Two tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt: A teaspoon of salt
  • Water: Just enough to make it smooth

Steps to make pasta

  1. Make the dough
  2. Turn the dough out and knead for 10 minutes
  3. Let the dough rest
  4. Divide the dough into balls
  5. Use a pasta machine to roll and cut the dough into desired pasta shapes
